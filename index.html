❓ How to play

🐦 Single player

  • Flap: click, tap, or press Space / ↑.
  • Fly through the gaps — hitting a pipe or the ground ends the run.
  • It gets harder as you score: faster pipes, smaller gaps, then moving and wide pipes.
  • Pick a Difficulty, Background, and Player (use a photo as your bird) below the game.

🌐 Multiplayer (race your friends)

Everyone flies the same pipes and sees each other live. Last bird flying wins (ties go to the higher score).

  1. Each player opens the game on their own device (same Vercel link) and picks their Player bird.
  2. Tap 🌐 Multiplayer and type a name.
  3. One person taps Create room and shares the room code (the copy button helps).
  4. Everyone else taps Join, enters that code, and waits in the room — you'll see each other in the player list.
  5. The host (who created the room) picks the Difficulty and taps Start race. A 3‑2‑1 countdown begins.
  6. Flap to survive! Others appear as faded ghost birds with name tags; the scoreboard (top-right) shows everyone's score.
  7. When the race ends, the host can tap Rematch, or anyone can Leave.

💡 Tips

  • Use the live Vercel link (not a downloaded file) so online play can connect.
  • Works best with friends on normal home/phone internet. Very locked-down office/school Wi‑Fi can sometimes block it.
  • The host's Difficulty applies to everyone in the race.
